Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and use third-party services at Artistic Tools Hub.

1. Introduction

Artistic Tools Hub (“we”, “us”, or “the Site”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, artistictoolshub.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message via Formspree, a third-party service.
  • Usage Data: Our third-party ad provider, Google AdSense, may collect anonymized data about your interactions with ads, such as clicks or impressions, using cookies or similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form.
  • Provide and improve our ad-supported services through Google AdSense.
  • Analyze site usage to enhance user experience (anonymized data only).

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdSense: To serve ads. AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads based on your interests. Learn more at Google’s Ad Policies.
  • Formspree: To process contact form submissions. Formspree’s privacy practices are available at Formspree’s Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Google AdSense uses cookies to deliver personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads by:

  • Visiting Google’s Ad Settings.
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block cookies (note: this may affect site functionality).

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no online service is 100% secure. Contact form data is stored securely by Formspree, and we do not store it ourselves.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU, California), you may have rights to:

  • Access or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of data processing for ads.

To exercise these rights, contact us via our Contact page. For AdSense data, use Google’s tools linked above.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here, and your continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, use our Contact page.
